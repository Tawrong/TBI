Thesis

Our thesis project focuses on developing an IoT-based system for monitoring and controlling light and temperature in a garlic research environment. By integrating sensors and actuators, our system provides real-time insights and automated adjustments to ensure optimal growth conditions for garlic.

Android Application

In addition to our IoT-based system, our thesis project involves the development of a mobile application tailored for garlic research. The app serves as a user-friendly interface for researchers to remotely monitor and control the environmental conditions of the research space.
